CNC and Vertical Mills

Our CNC and vertical milling machines are employed to sculpt and craft a diverse range of materials, providing consistent quality and guaranteed lead times for all your part requirements, making us your single-source CNC parts supplier.

Wire EDM Machines

Our wire EDM machines use a metallic wire to cut or shape a workpiece, often a conductive material, with a thin electrode wire that follows a precisely programmed path.

Grinders

Grinder machines are instruments employed to attain exceptional precision and surface quality in a variety of materials like metals, ceramics, plastics, and others, by removing material from a workpiece through abrasion, typically with a rotating abrasive wheel.
