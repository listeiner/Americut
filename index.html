Tools, Dies, Excellence

Americut Corporation is a tool and die shop located in Meadville, PA, that specialize in the meticulous engineering, innovative design, accurate construction, and rigorous validation of exceptionally precise metal stamping dies and tooling system solutions. Our dedicated team of experts collaborates with industry leaders to deliver cutting-edge tooling solutions tailored for high-strength materials, exacting tolerances, and expedited lead times.

Our services include designing and building progressive dies, extrusion dies, plastic molds, sub-contract machining work and more! We are experienced in all materials d-2, carbide, stainless, graphite, and ceramics.

CNC and Vertical Mills

Our CNC and vertical milling machines are employed to sculpt and craft a diverse range of materials, providing consistent quality and guaranteed lead times for all your part requirements, making us your single-source CNC parts supplier.

Wire EDM Machines

Our wire EDM machines use a metallic wire to cut or shape a workpiece, often a conductive material, with a thin electrode wire that follows a precisely programmed path.

Grinders

Grinder machines are instruments employed to attain exceptional precision and surface quality in a variety of materials like metals, ceramics, plastics, and others, by removing material from a workpiece through abrasion, typically with a rotating abrasive wheel.
